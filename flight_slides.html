Flight Dataset Exploration Presentation¶

by Esraa Ahmed¶

Investigate Overview¶

In this investigation on the US Flights for 2008, I will focus on: Arrival and Departual Times, Cancellation and Delay Reasons.

Dataset Overview¶

Flight Dataset of the year 2008, contains various columns about delays and cancellation of the flights. Data Source: https://community.amstat.org/jointscsg-section/dataexpo/dataexpo2009

labels = 'Weather', 'Carrier','National Air System', 'Security'
plt.subplots(figsize=(14,6))
sorted_counts = flight['CancellationCode'].value_counts()
plt.title('Cancellation Reasons', fontsize=(20))
explode = (0.1, 0.1, 0.1,0.1)
plt.pie(sorted_counts ,labels= labels,autopct='%1.1f%%',startangle=90, shadow = True, explode=explode)
plt.show()
  • The most reasons of cancellation are Weather and Carrier.
